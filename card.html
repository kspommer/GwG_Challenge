Lucky the Dog

Lucky, once scared to go more than three blocks from her home, is now an avid walker and loves to chase squirrels en route.

  • Breed: Minature poodle
  • Color: Black
  • Age: 3 years old
  • Favorite toy: Brown stuffed moose

Lucky grew up in an urban high rise apartment, rarely spending time outdoors. When we started walking, she was terrified to leave her house. Each day we could venture a bit further. Now, she equates my name with a walk and doesn't even look back when we leave. We both have a lot of fun on our adventures!
